Over the past two years, there has been a decline in social interaction between local communities worldwide due to the covid-19 pandemic. While many families are self-sufficient in taking care of themselves, many single-parent families and older residents have had their lives impacted by a magnitude of extra chores, limited financial income and uncertainty about the future. HelpOut plans to change that by providing an easy to use digital platform for individuals amongst a local community to express tasks they need support with. Additionally the platform allows generic posts to be uploaded or the organising of social events to bring the community closer together.
The thing that makes HelpOut unique and special is it focuses on smaller local communities by only showing events or assistance required within their post code.
